Namdaemun Market

Walk 15–18 min from Myeongdong. Top 5 for a group of 10 — the most fun, most Korean, easiest to explore: Kalguksu Alley, Sock Street, Kids' Clothing & Toy Street, Street Food Stalls, Kitchenware Zone.

The full Namdaemun map (10 zones)
  • Kalguksu Alley 칼국수 골목 — the iconic experience: knife-cut noodles, dumplings, spicy seafood soup, kimchi pancakes. Loud, chaotic, delicious. Must-try: kalguksu + mandu combo.
  • Grilled Fish Alley 생선구이 골목 — charcoal-grilled fish; smoky, fast, cheap, flavourful.
  • Kids' Clothing & Toy Street — cute, cheap, surprisingly good quality. Great for gifts.
  • Kitchenware & Homeware Zone — where locals buy authentic everyday Korean home goods.
  • Sock Street 양말 골목 — Koreans rate this HIGH; cute, durable socks at ₩1,000–2,000 a pair.
  • Bag & Luggage Street — excellent prices, lots of choice.
  • Street food stalls — hotteok (Namdaemun's is famous), tteokbokki, mandu, tornado potato, egg bread, corn dogs.
  • Traditional snack shops — yakgwa, hangwa, dried seaweed, walnut cookies, rice crackers. Perfect souvenirs.
  • Fabric & Sewing Street — huge and very local.
  • Sunglasses & Accessories Street — great deals, great for teens.
